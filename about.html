About us

Odysse, a London-based company in the transportation sector, pioneers the provision of electric vehicle fleets as a service to taxi and private hire operators. With a core focus on sustainability and technology-driven solutions, Odysse holds significant contracts and exhibits scalable operations, poised to disrupt the urban transportation landscape and advance the transition to electric mobility. As the inaugural electric vehicle (EV) fleet-as-a-service provider in London, Odysse is dedicated to reshaping urban transportation while tackling pressing environmental and social issues.

At the heart of Odysse's mission is the transformation of urban mobility through the provision of sustainable, low-carbon solutions that contribute to the global Net-Zero agenda. Leveraging innovative business models and state-of-the-art technology, Odysse endeavors to forge a cleaner, more efficient, and socially responsible transportation ecosystem.

Aligned with the Net-Zero agenda, Odysse operates as a sustainable, low-carbon, and socially responsible enterprise. By supplying electric vehicles and curtailing emissions in the transportation sector, Odysse actively contributes to environmental and social objectives.
