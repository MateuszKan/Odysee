About us

Odysse, a London-based company that has pioneered the provision of an all-electric vehicle, fleet-as-a-service to ride hailing and private-hire operators, is rapidly reshaping the existing urban transportation landscape. With its core focus on sustainability, scalable operations, and technology-driven solutions, Odysse is deeply invested in the transformation of urban mobility through the delivery of sustainable, low-carbon solutions that contribute to the global Net-Zero agenda.

Not only is Odysse contributing to environmental objectives by expanding the use of electric vehicles, it is also using an employment model that allows its driver employees flexibility, sustained pay, and work-life balance.

With significant relationships with key partners, innovative business models and state-of-the-art technology, Odysse has created its own unique paradigm, one that gives London commuters a cleaner, more efficient, and socially responsible transportation ecosystem.
